OUR PERSPECTIVE

We've set out to be more than a contracting company.

Rimajin exists because we believe the most resilient energy solutions are found by returning to the fundamentals.

The Humble Approach

Title and tenure are common in this industry, but they aren't the mission. We believe that true expertise is crafted in the quiet discipline of doing a job well. We approach every solar array, EV hub, and service call with the humility of a student and the precision of a seasoned hand.

Our focus is narrow by design. By specializing in the intersection of sustainable generation and modern mobility, we ensure our experience is deep. We will navigate your unique situation by being the most thoughtful partner for your energy transition.

Community Interaction

Beyond the tools, we care about the people of New Braunfels. Power is something we all share, yet few truly understand. Our goal is to bridge that gap through local workshops and a collective of goods that reflect our values of efficiency and stewardship.
